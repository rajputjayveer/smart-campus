Implementation: Backend cron job queries orders and feedbacks tables, uses simple ML (e.g., Prophet.js or TensorFlow.js) to predict trends. Expose via /api/forecast and show in ShopPanel dashboard. Store predictions in a new MySQL table.
Essential for optimizing stock in food delivery.
